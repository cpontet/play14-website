Our code of conduct

  • You shall always be open minded and ready to listen and learn from others
  • You shall feel free to propose, experiment and explain what you have in mind
  • You shall not high-jack, sabotage or ruin the learning experience of other people
  • You shall mind your own timebox when facilitating a game so that other sessions can start on time
  • You shall behave appropriately and act as a decent human being
  • You shall be ready to get serious fun and good laughs, and should not be shy about that

To summarize : "You shall not be an a**hole".
